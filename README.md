## 🏛️ The Sovereign Engine (The Economics)
Earthspan is not a "tax" on environmental action; it is a permanent funding engine. Utilizing the **Transfer Fee Extension**, the protocol captures **programmatic value** from every trade. 

This value is automatically directed to five critical pillars of recovery:

1.  **The Field 🟢:** Direct **Atomic Payouts** to the Burwa Bosses on the ground.
2.  **The Assets 🗺️:** A **Land Acquisition Fund** to purchase and protect critical titles.
3.  **The Resilience 🛡️:** **Resource Resilience** initiatives for local community security (food/water).
4.  **The Future 🚀:** An **Innovation Fund** for satellite/sensor R&D and data verification.
5.  **The Ecosystem 🏦:** The **Earthspan Treasury** for operational scaling.

## 🛡️ "Institutional-Grade Safu" Architecture
We manage **Key Man Risk** and **Inflation** through sophisticated on-chain governance:

* **Adaptive Fee Management:** Protocol fees are **Modular** (amendable). We can tune the Sovereign Engine to balance ecological impact and market liquidity.
* **Multisig Control:** All critical administrative authorities—including the **Update Authority** for the Sovereign Engine—are held by a **Squads Multisig**, not a single private key.
* **Transferable Authority:** Control of the protocol can be seamlessly transferred to a third-party custodian or a legal DAO.
