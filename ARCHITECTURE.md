# $UPRA Protocol Architecture

## 1. Asset Layer (RWA)
* **Standard:** Solana Token Extensions (Token-2022).
* **Asset:** "Impact Metres" (Unique land parcels).
* **Verification:** Decentralized Oracles linking physical growth to on-chain metadata.

## 2. Financial Layer (Treasury)
* **Token:** $UPRA (Fungible Liquid Impact).
* **Mechanism:** Automated yield distribution via Transfer Hooks.
* **Liquidity:** Constant-product pools for institutional entry/exit.

## 3. Operations Layer (Burwa)
* **Identity:** Burwa Boss IDs (Soulbound tokens for local leaders).
* **Incentives:** 1% automated royalty on all $UPRA secondary volume.
  
## The $UPRA "Finance-to-Field" Pipeline

### 1. Institutional Entry (Land Acquisition)
Institutional capital enters the protocol to mint $UPRA. These funds are used to secure land titles and provide initial logistics for recovery efforts.

### 2. Secondary Market Velocity (The Burwa Royalty)
A core innovation of the protocol is the **Transfer Hook**. Every secondary trade of $UPRA on global markets automatically captures a 1% "Recovery Fee." 
* **The Impact:** This creates a perpetual, high-velocity income stream for Burwa Bosses, independent of traditional grant cycles or government delays.

### 3. Verification & Yield (The Oracle Loop)
Ecological recovery is verified via Satellite Oracles. 
* **The Result:** Confirmed biomass growth triggers the distribution of Yield (USDC) back to $UPRA holders, aligning institutional profit with the physical recovery of the planet.
